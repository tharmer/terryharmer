Live · AI product

PolicyProof

A second-opinion check on insurance claim denials for independent agencies. It reads the full policy, tests the carrier's denial against the actual coverage and the state's unfair-claims statutes, and drafts a pushback letter when the denial doesn't hold up. The guardrails are the whole point: every policy line it quotes is verified in code against the source document, it's blocked from citing any law it can't confirm, the agent always makes the final call, and nothing is ever stored.
